Z O Y A   B A W A N G A O N W A L A

Hello! My name is Zoya. I am a junior at the Early College at Guilford, and I am passionate about art and digital graphic design. Over the years I have also been developing an interest in computer science, and I am working towards deepening my understanding of coding and integrating it with my skills in graphics. As I continue to learn and grow, I created this site to track my progress and expand my reach within my community. This site is a highlight reel of my artwork and coding projects. A R T   P O R T F O L I O


   

From a very young age, I've always had a love for art. I'm mostly self-taught, but I have taken some fine arts classes to develop my skill in mediums like charcoal, pastels, watercolors, and even oils. My main focus is in portraiture and photorealism with pencil drawings, but my styles range from drawings to paintings as well. I also do a lot of digital work using Adobe Photoshop and Procreate to create graphics on larger scales.
